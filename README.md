This is a chatbot that simulate to be an engineering companion which will provide support on your carreer.
This chatbot will have knowledge of all assignments and their particular documentation.
It can:
- Provide a quick summary of any topic.
- Provide information about schedules regarding classes and exams.
- It can provide quick exams checks.
